About Game Tracker

Welcome to Game Tracker, your one-stop destination for tracking and discovering video games. This platform was designed with the gaming community in mind, providing easy access to both paid and free games.

  • Wishlist: Create and manage your personal game wishlist.
  • Explore Paid Games: Explore a wide range of paid games, filtering by price range.
  • Discover Free Games: Discover popular free games, sorted by release date, platform, relevance, and popularity.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a hardcore enthusiast, Game Tracker is here to enhance your gaming experience.
